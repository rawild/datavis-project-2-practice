Who Pays?
Political Contributions to New York State Politicians 2015-2020
This data looks at the donations to state politicians representing the five boroughs of New York City from January 2015 to January 2020. Statewide public officials were also included, including Andrea Stewart-Cousins, the Majority Leader of the Senate, and the four statewide offices: Governor, Lieutenant Governor, Attorney General, and Comptroller.
